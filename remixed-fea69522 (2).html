my letter to you

My dearest Aashna,

I don't really know how to put everything I feel into words, but I wanted to try — even if this whole page is just my clumsy way of saying something very simple: you are the best part of my life.

There's a version of me from before you that I genuinely can't picture as fully happy. Not because nothing was good back then, but because I didn't know yet what it felt like to have someone who makes the ordinary days feel like something worth remembering. You did that. You still do that, every single day.

I love the way you laugh at things before you've even finished the joke. I love how easy it is to talk to you about absolutely nothing for hours and somehow it never gets boring. I love that you make me want to be better — more patient, more present, more myself — just by being around you.

I'm not always great at saying this out loud, so I wanted it written somewhere permanent, somewhere you can come back to whenever you need a reminder: you are loved, completely and without conditions, by someone who notices the little things about you and treasures every one of them.

Thank you for choosing me, again and again, on the easy days and the hard ones. Thank you for being patient with me when I get it wrong, and for staying anyway. I don't take a single bit of that for granted.

Whatever comes next for us, I want you to know I'm not going anywhere. I'd choose you again in every version of this life, every time, without hesitation.

I love you, Aashna. More than this letter, or this silly little website, could ever really say.

— always yours, Jenil
